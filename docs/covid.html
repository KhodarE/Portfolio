Coronavirus Monitoring Mobile Application (iOS and Android)


Coronavirus Monitoring Mobile Application was developed during my 12 month contract with Western Sydney University. The Coronavirus application was developed for research purposes and provide its clients with safety measures to protect them during the ever-growing pandemic.


The Coronavirus Monitoring application consists of:

- Register/Login Account

- Fill out Account Details

- Share location

- Shows hotspot locations on a map

- Sends notifications every 2-4 hours reminding users to wash their hands, sanitise and wear a mask

- Frequently Asked Questions page

- Clients can send questions and be provided feedback


Framework: Xcode and Android Studios

Programming Language: Java, XML, C++, Swift

Position: Full Stack Mobile Developer (Front-end and Back-end)

Duration: 6 months

Completion: 100% completed

Feedback: The final product was well received by the clients and will be used for testing starting in 2021.
