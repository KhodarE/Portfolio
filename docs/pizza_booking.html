Gourmet Pizza Booking


Gourmet Pizza Booking is a Website that provides indiviudals wanting to book pizza virtually through the use of a mobile phone or computer.

The pizza booking site consists of:

- Register/Login Account

- Clients can edit their account details

- Administrator can manage all the pizza bookings made on the website

- Administrator can view all pizza booking statistics

- Clients can search for all available pizzas

- Clients can order one or multiple pizzas

- Clients can either edit, view or delete their pizza booking if they change their mind


Framework: ASP.NET

Programming Language: HTML, CSS, Java, C#, jQuery

Position: Full Stack Team Leader (Front-end and Back-end)

Duration: 2 months

Completion: 100% completed

Feedback: The final product was well received by the clients.
