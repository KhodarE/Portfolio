HAWMAE Web Application


Health and Well-being Mobile Application or also known as HAWMAE. Is a Web Application that evaluates Mobile Applications that are available to the public on the marketplace, through the use of Projects and Surveys. Through the feedback provided by completed surveys it'll give HAWMAE an inisght on how to evaluate the application based on the users Engagement, Functionality, Aesthetics, Information, Subjective and App Specific questions. Through the results they can be further research and analysis made to determine if the application is safe for society to use and if it really provides a solution that is being advertised.


The HAWMAE system consists of:

- Register/Login and Request Account

- CRUD Projects and Surveys

- Categorised Health Categories

- Categorised Survey Questions

- View data in Line/Column/Pie charts

- Download survey questions, answers and score averages in excel files

- Inviting individuals to complete surveys

- Administrator can modify account statuses and review Requested Accounts


Framework: Laravel

Programming Language: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP

Position: Full Stack Web Developer (Front-end and Back-end)

Duration: 6 months

Completion: 100% completed

Feedback: The final product was well received by the clients and the system will be published to be used in early 2021 for research.
